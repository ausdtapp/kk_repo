KK program functionality

How to use:
    
    1. use x = create_matches() to create a dictionary of matches.

    2. use check_matches(x) to check your matches against the previous two years

    3. use save_current_match() to save your current match in the current directory

    4. use send_current_matches() to send the matches saved in your directory

    5. after the year is finished use conclude_year() to archive your current matches into the previous_years folder
